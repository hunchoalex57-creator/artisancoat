Our Story

20+ Years of Surfaces Done Right

Artisan Coat was built on a simple belief: that most surfaces don't need to be torn out — they need the right craftsman with the right process. Over two decades working across the Greater Toronto Area, we've restored hundreds of porches, pool decks, patios, and interior floors for homeowners who were told "you need to replace it."

We showed them a better way.

What separates us isn't just the finish — it's what happens before it. Identifying the issue correctly and preparing the surface properly is the most critical part of any job. A weak base means a failing finish, no matter what product goes on top. We've seen it time and time again: clients who call us after another company rushed through the prep, used the wrong material, or simply didn't understand the surface they were working on. We come in, diagnose it properly, and fix it right.

We work with multiple systems — decorative concrete overlays, microcement, concrete repairs, sealing, staining — and we choose the right one for each specific situation. Not every surface needs the same solution, and experience is what tells you which one to use.

Our clients consistently rate us 5 stars — not just for the results, but for how we work: on time, communicative, respectful of your home, and priced honestly.
