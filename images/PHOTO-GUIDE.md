# Artisan Coat — Photo Placement Guide

Supported formats: `.jpg` or `.webp` (preferred for speed).
Recommended: resize to max 1400px wide, compress to under 300KB per image.

## TIPS

- **Before/After pairs**: crop both photos at the same angle so the transformation is clear.
- **Hero images**: shoot landscape (wide), min 1400×800px.
- **Portrait/sidebar images**: shoot portrait or square, min 800×1000px.
- **WhatsApp tip**: you can send photos directly via WhatsApp to show clients. The same photos work here.
- **WebP format**: if your phone/editor supports it, save as `.webp` — it loads faster. Just rename the extension in the filename above.
