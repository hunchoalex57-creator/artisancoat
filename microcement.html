What Is Microcement?

The Seamless Surface That Changes Everything

Microcement is a polymer-enhanced cement coating applied in ultra-thin layers — typically 2–3mm — over any existing surface. The result is a perfectly smooth, continuous finish with no grout lines, no tile joints, and no seams of any kind.

It's the finish you see in high-end boutiques, luxury hotels, and architectural showrooms. Now it's available for homes and commercial spaces across the GTA — applied by craftsmen who have been working with this material for years.

And the best part: it goes directly over your existing tile, concrete, or plywood subfloor. No demolition. No dumpster. Just a brand new surface in days, not weeks.

Professional System

A 6-Layer Application System

Microcement isn't just slapped on. It's a multi-layer system that builds durability into every coat.

1
Base Surface

Your existing tile, concrete, or subfloor — properly cleaned and primed for adhesion.

2
Anti-Crack Layer

Special primer with glass fibre mesh applied to prevent surface cracking over time.

3
Leveling Layer

Microcement base coat scraped evenly and sanded smooth to create a flat, uniform base.

4
Effect Layer

The decorative microcement coat applied by hand — this creates the final color and texture.

5
Waterproof Sealing Layer

Primer rolled again to lock in the finish and create a waterproof barrier.

6
Protective Topcoat

Two-component protective topcoat rolled evenly — the final shield against wear, staining, and moisture.

Questions

Microcement FAQ

Can microcement go over existing tile?+

Yes — in most cases, microcement can be applied directly over existing ceramic, porcelain, or stone tile. We prepare the surface properly to ensure adhesion, which eliminates the need for demolition entirely. This is one of the biggest cost and time savings microcement offers.

Is microcement waterproof?+

Yes. When sealed with our two-component protective topcoat, microcement is fully waterproof. It's suitable for bathrooms, showers, kitchens, and wet areas. The sealed surface also makes it extremely easy to clean — just wipe it down.

How long does microcement installation take?+

A typical bathroom or small room takes 3–5 days. Larger floors or commercial spaces may take 5–10 days depending on size and complexity. Each layer needs to dry fully before the next is applied — we never rush this process.

What colors are available?+

Microcement is available in a wide range of colors — from warm creams and off-whites to cool charcoals and mid-tone grays. We can also create custom tones. The finish can be matte, satin, or gloss depending on your preference.

Is microcement durable enough for floors?+

Absolutely. When properly applied and sealed, microcement is highly abrasion-resistant and handles heavy foot traffic well. We've installed it in commercial spaces, boutiques, and busy family kitchens — all holding up years later.
