Decorative Concrete & Resurfacing

We use polymer-modified decorative concrete overlay systems applied directly over your existing surface. First we diagnose the condition, repair all cracks and structural damage, then apply a stamped decorative finish in your choice of stone, tile, or brick pattern and color. The right product is chosen based on the specific surface — not a one-size-fits-all approach.

Works on: porches, stairs, pool decks, patios, walkways, driveways, and retaining walls. Frost-resistant and built for Canadian winters.

  • ✓ Proper surface diagnosis before any work begins
  • ✓ All cracks and structural issues repaired first
  • ✓ Any color — any stamped pattern
  • ✓ Saves 40–60% vs. full replacement
  • ✓ 3-year warranty

Interior & Exterior

Microcement Installation

Microcement is a seamless, ultra-thin coating that turns any surface into a continuous, grout-free finish. Applied by hand over existing floors, walls, or counters — no demo required. The result is a smooth, modern aesthetic you can't achieve with tile.

Works on: floors, walls, bathrooms, kitchens, feature walls, fireplaces, stairs, and commercial spaces.

  • ✓ Seamless — no grout, no joints
  • ✓ Applied over existing tile or concrete
  • ✓ Waterproof, abrasion & stain resistant
  • ✓ Luxury finish at a fraction of tile cost
  • ✓ Residential and commercial

Repair & Protection

Concrete Repairs, Sealing & Staining

Not every surface needs a full decorative overlay. Sometimes the right move is a targeted concrete repair, a quality sealer, or a concrete stain to refresh the look and protect the surface. We assess what the surface actually needs — and we never oversell.

We've also corrected countless jobs where another contractor skipped repairs and went straight to the coating — causing the finish to crack, peel, or delaminate. Proper concrete repair is the foundation of everything.

  • ✓ Structural crack and spall repair
  • ✓ Edge rebuilding and surface leveling
  • ✓ Concrete sealing — penetrating & topical
  • ✓ Concrete staining for a fresh, clean look
  • ✓ Extends surface life significantly
